About

Our Venezuelan arepas are handmade the traditional way. The arepa is a corn based flat round “bread” that we make from scratch. It is a round patty that is grilled, baked or fried. Once fully cooked, it is split open and stuffed with our homemade signature ingredients. To enjoy our arepas in the way it was meant to be, YOU need to hold it in your hands and eat it like a sandwich. Below, you will find a few of our staple menu items; we can modify or combine ingredients. It may imply additional charge.
